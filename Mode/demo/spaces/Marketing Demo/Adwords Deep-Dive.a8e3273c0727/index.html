Adwords Deep-Dive

_________________________________________________________________

What has happened?


On November 10th '19, Brian (PM) noticed a sudden spike in the website traffic. The new traffic was from the Adwords channel. However, these new website landings are not resulting in Free Signups. In fact, the Sign-up rate has dropped from 9.7% to 2.8%

Why has it happened?


We've identified the new Adwords messaging to be the likely reason for this anomaly:

  • Starting November 3, we've launched a new Adwords campaign focused on 'Workflow'. Being the biggest campaign till date, it has generated a lot of traction leading to a high spike in website traffic. However, the signup rate for the new campaign is only 2.8%.
  • To put this number in perspective, compare it with the signup rates of our previous campaigns - 'Governance' (5.8%), 'Collaboration' (12.17%) or (even the second worst performing keyword) 'Speed' (4.09%)

Note: We've confirmed that there is no changing in our target demographics for the new campaign

What are the financial implications for the business?


Not only is the new campaign performing poor from a Signup perspective, it is also costing the business a ton of money. For reference:

  • We've spent $10.5k on the campaign so far and have only seen revenue of $5400. This has resulted in a net loss of ~$5.2k
  • To put this number in perspective, the second worst campaign resulted in a net loss of just $2,111


Note: We're calculating revenue by computing an estimated Lifetime Value of each new signup
