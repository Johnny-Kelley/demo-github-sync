CUSTOMER INSIGHTS REPORT

The objective of this report is to uncover hidden trends in customer purchases (Exploratory Data Analysis)


The dataset used for this report contains a random sample of over 32 million orders from more than 200,000 customers. For each user, 4 to 100 of their prior orders are given, with the sequence of products purchased in each order. All orders made were between January to March 2019

EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS
  • Seems like Sunday evenings and Monday mornings are the busiest
  • Almost 95% of the orders being placed between 8 AM and 9 PM, the peak ordering hour being 10 AM - 12 PM
