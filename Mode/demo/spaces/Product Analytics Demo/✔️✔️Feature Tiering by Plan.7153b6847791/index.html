Feature Tiering by Plan

This report aims to provide insights on feature tiering between a Tier 1, Tier 2 and Free version of Zoomi. By examining current product interactions by SMBs and Enterprises, we aim to answer the following questions:

  • Is a given product feature valuable?
  • Will there be a large delta in the value experienced by Tier 1 and Tier 2 customers?

ï»¿

Then we use the following general guide to recommend whether the feature should be Tier 1 or Tier 2.



---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Meeting Invitation by Non-Meeting Creator

The ability to send a meeting invitation by someone other than the meeting creator.
  • Meeting Invitations by Non-Meeting Creator are highly valuable to both SMB as well as Enterprise customers.


  • The median customer for has over 2 manual invitations by a non-Meeting creator for every meeting created



Recommendation: Tier 2

Opportunity: Drive SMBs to Tier 2 for this valuable features
Risk: Low adoption of Free due to lack of value

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Screen Sharing by Meeting Creator

The ability to share a screen by the meeting creator.
  • Screen Sharing is a fairly niche activity for both SMB and Enterprise customers, suggesting there is not a widespread value.


  • It is more valued by SMB customers. The median SMB customer uses twice as often as the median Enterprise customer.



Recommendation: FREE

Opportunity: Differentiate from other free tools
Risk: Low interest

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Chatting within the Meeting Window

The ability to chat with meeting participants
  • Chatting is valuable to both Standard and Plus customers.






Recommendation: FREE (Limited)

Opportunity: Drive adoption of FREE
Risk: Cannibalize Tier 2

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Recording the Meeting

The ability for Meeting Creator to record the proceedings
  • Recoding is used infrequently by both SMB and Enterprise customers.


  • It is slightly more valued by Enterprise customers.




Recommendation: FREE

Opportunity: Differentiate from other free tools and provide some value for Meeting Creators who care about accountability
Risk: Decrease appeal for Enterprise
