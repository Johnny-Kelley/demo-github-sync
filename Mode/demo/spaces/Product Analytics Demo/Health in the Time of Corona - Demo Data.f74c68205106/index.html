Methodology:


Customer health scores are bucketed into three categories, Firm, Account and Product Usage Health each with its own health score determined by scores assigned to attributes within each category

Firm

  1. Industry health
  2. Funding health
  3. Revenue health

Account

  1. Account penetration (Active seats/# Employees)
  2. Time as customer
  3. Product Feature usage (“Four By Four”) (Schedules, email subs, 5+ spaces, custom colors)
  4. CSM Assessment of Renewal Risk
  5. CSM Assessment of Customer Engagement

Product Usage

  1. DAU/MAU
  2. MAU/Active Seats (“Rolling 28-day Engagement Rate)
  3. Seat penetration


Each of these three scores are normalized to each have the same weight in determining the overall health score.

The sum of these three scores is the overall health score.

Scoring:

Each attribute is assigned a risk score on a scale of 1 to 3, with 1 representing the lowest risk and 3 highest risk. Details on the calculation of attribute scores can be found in the documentation linked above
